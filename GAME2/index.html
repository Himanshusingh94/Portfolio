How to Play Boost Your Memory

  1. Press "Start Game" to begin.
  2. The game will show you a sequence of flashing buttons.
  3. Remember the sequence and click the buttons in the *exact same order*.
  4. Each correct sequence increases your level and score.
  5. If you make a mistake, the game is over.
  6. Choose a difficulty (Easy, Medium, Hard) to change the sequence speed.
  7. Your High Score is saved locally!
